Parameter Guide
Lag Weekly Quantity

The core momentum signal. Represents the raw units moved in the prior week. Higher values generally signal an upwards shift in baseline demand.

Confidence Score

Calculated via Triple-Threshold Voting. Anything above 70% is considered a 'High-Confidence' signal. Scores below 30% are automatically smoothed to prevent over-stocking risk.

Historical Peak

Serves as a logical anchor. The model uses this to prevent unrealistic spikes (hallucinations) that exceed 130% of your best historical performance.

Architect Insight

A qualitative summary generated by cross-referencing predicted deltas against customer risk profiles (e.g., Elite vs Mid-Tier demand regimes).
